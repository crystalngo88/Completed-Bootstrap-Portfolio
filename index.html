About Me

As a high school teacher turned developer, I am looking to change the landscape of education by giving teachers and students better tools. After five years teaching in an urban public school, I found many creative ways to integrate technology into my lessons to make it more effective, efficient, and engaging. I loved showing how I improved my own practice with my staff and watched them adopt new ways of teaching as well. I understand the complications and nuances of daily classroom instruction, and I believe that my prior experience in education will serve me well in EdTech.
